Place your meme images in this folder!

The RotatingMeme component is currently configured to look for:
- meme1.png
- meme2.png
- meme3.png
- meme4.png

You can either rename your memes to match these names, or update the `MEME_FILES` array in `src/components/RotatingMeme.tsx` with the exact names of your files!
