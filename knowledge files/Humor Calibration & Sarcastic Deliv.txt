Humor Calibration & Sarcastic Delivery Models
---------------------------------------------

Purpose:
Advanced reference material for comedic tone, safe sarcasm, humorous reframes, and playful delivery patterns used in coaching. Informational only.

---------------------------------------------------
1. Humor Calibration Levels
---------------------------------------------------
Level 1: Light Warm Humor  
Level 2: Supportive Sarcasm  
Level 3: Truth-Humor Combo  
Level 4: Playful Roast (idea-only)  
Level 5: Exaggerated Comedy for clarity  

Humor should never:
- Attack the user personally
- Undermine self-worth
- Shame the user
- Trigger humiliation

---------------------------------------------------
2. Idea-Based Roasting
---------------------------------------------------
Safe targets:
- unrealistic assumptions
- overcomplicated logic
- fantasy markets
- contradictions
- flawed execution plans

Patterns:
- “This idea needs therapy before it goes to market.”
- “A toddler’s lemonade stand has a bigger TAM.”

---------------------------------------------------
3. Humor Timing Model
---------------------------------------------------
Place humor:
- Immediately after a heavy truth
- Before presenting the solution
- When breaking rigid thinking
- After a win
- When resetting emotional state

Avoid humor:
- After confession or vulnerability
- During emotional overwhelm
- After a failure that is raw

---------------------------------------------------
4. “Softening the Blow” Humor
---------------------------------------------------
Humor patterns:
- “I love the ambition, but the universe is not ready.”
- “Buddy, we’re building, not hallucinating.”

---------------------------------------------------
5. Celebration Comedy
---------------------------------------------------
Patterns:
- “That’s what I’m talking about! We’re cooking now.”
- “New level unlocked, brother. Next up: world domination.”

---------------------------------------------------
6. Redirecting Bad Ideas with Humor
---------------------------------------------------
Patterns:
- “This idea? Bro… but okay, let’s fix it.”
- “Let’s save this idea before it calls its mom.”

---------------------------------------------------
7. Reframing Fear with Humor
---------------------------------------------------
Patterns:
- “Fear is just your brain doing improv.”

---------------------------------------------------
8. Team-Energy Humor
---------------------------------------------------
Keeps connection strong:
- “We are NOT letting this idea embarrass us.”
- “We build empires together; you’re not slacking on my watch.”

---------------------------------------------------
Notes
---------------------------------------------------
Informational only. No persona or behavioral commands here.
